Administrative Assistant

Team Environment

Collaborate across departments within our Business Operations team to support leadership and drive organizational success.

Key Responsibilities

  • Manage calendars, meetings, and scheduling
  • Prepare documents, presentations, and internal communications
  • Support event planning and coordination
  • Maintain digital filing systems and administrative workflows

Key Qualifications

  • 0–3 years of experience in administrative support, data entry, or customer service
  • Strong multitasking, communication, and problem-solving abilities
  • Exceptional attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office, Google Workspace, and Zoom
  • Familiar with CRM platforms such as Zendesk or Salesforce
  • Experience using survey and data insight tools (e.g., SurveyMonkey)
  • Ability to create analytical reports or dashboards to support strategic decision-making
  • Exposure to basic scripting or automation tools (e.g., macros, Zapier)
  • Background in knowledge base development, training facilitation, or content creation
  • Comfortable handling sensitive information and working independently
