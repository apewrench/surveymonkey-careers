Open Remote Roles

We’re seeking sharp, detail-oriented professionals to join our team in one of the following key roles:

  • Customer Support Specialist
  • Data Entry Analyst
  • Administrative Assistant

Customer Support Specialist

Your Future Team: Work with our dynamic, customer-obsessed Support team to resolve issues and deliver exceptional service.

What You'll Do:

  • Respond to customer inquiries via email, chat, and other channels
  • Troubleshoot and escalate complex issues
  • Maintain detailed records
  • Collaborate with product and engineering teams
  • Contribute to support documentation

What We're Looking For:

  • 0–3 years in customer support
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Problem-solving mindset
  • Familiarity with CRM tools (Zendesk, Salesforce, etc.)
  • Ability to work independently

Data Entry Analyst

Your Future Team: Support our Operations and Analytics team by maintaining and organizing large datasets that drive business decisions.

What You'll Do:

  • Enter, update, and verify data
  • Perform audits for accuracy
  • Generate reports and visualizations
  • Collaborate with analysts
  • Maintain data confidentiality

What We're Looking For:

  • 0–3 years in data entry or admin support
  • High attention to detail
  • Proficiency in Excel, Google Sheets
  • Strong organizational skills
  • Remote work readiness

Administrative Assistant

Your Future Team: Collaborate across departments in our Business Operations team to support leaders and drive organizational success.

What You'll Do:

  • Manage calendars and meetings
  • Prepare documents and presentations
  • Handle internal communications
  • Support event planning
  • Maintain digital filing systems

What We're Looking For:

  • 0–3 years in admin or executive support
  • Excellent multitasking and communication
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office, Google Workspace, Zoom
  • Confidentiality and independence

Professional Opportunity Statement

We are seeking talented individuals with a passion for data-driven decision-making and customer engagement. Ideal candidates will demonstrate:

  • Proficiency with survey and data-insights tools such as SurveyMonkey
  • Familiarity with basic scripting or automation platforms (e.g., macros, Zapier)
  • Experience in generating analytical reports or dashboards to support strategic initiatives
  • A background in knowledge-base development, training facilitation, or content creation

We understand that not every applicant will meet every qualification listed. At SurveyMonkey, we value diverse experiences and encourage individuals from all backgrounds to apply. Whether your career path is traditional or unconventional, your unique perspective is welcome here.
